Rules

Round 1 & 2

These rounds allow you to bank money. Decide whether you want to bank your money before answering each question. Answering questions correctly & consecutively will let you climb the money tree, while a wrong answer will reset your money tree to 0. The maximum amount of money you can bank in round 1 is $500 000, round 2 is $1 000 000.

Round 3

This is a sudden death round to win it all!!! You will have 60 seconds to answer 10 questions, all of which you must answer correct in order to win the money you have in the bank, otherwise you will be deemed The Weakest Link!!!
